WorldCupTravelDealsAPI integration guide

Build a World Cup 2026 host-city comparison

Help travelers compare destinations without pretending planning estimates are live prices or sending ticket buyers toward unsafe resale sources.

This API provides planning data, official links, provider search routes, and heuristic reference estimates. It does not return live fare, hotel, or ticket inventory.

Load the host-city catalog

Start with all cities or filter by a region to build destination comparison pages for the United States, Mexico, or Canada. 

GET /cities
GET /cities?region=Mexico

Compare the signals that matter

  • Expected demand and tourism tier
  • Stadium, airports, region, and time zone
  • Flight, hotel, and local-spend reference tiers
  • Match-window dates and safe official ticket links

Generate a planning route

Use a traveler origin, destination city, trip date, nights, and party size to generate provider search links and reference estimates. 

GET /deals/search?origin=EZE&city=miami&start=2026-06-15&nights=5&travelers=2

Label estimates correctly

Package candidates include is_live_offer: false and pricing_type: heuristic_reference. Preserve these disclosures in your application. When you have a real offer, send its price and your chosen baseline to /score. 

GET /score?price=1900&baseline=2400&safety=official_or_direct

Route ticket demand safely

Use /official-links to direct buyers to FIFA official ticketing, hospitality, and resale guidance. Avoid presenting unofficial ticket inventory as verified.
