Questions buyers ask

Straight answers before you subscribe

Where do I purchase and authenticate?

Subscribe through the relevant RapidAPI listing. RapidAPI handles plan selection and customer authentication.

Can I call the Render backend directly?

No. Product endpoints require a private marketplace gateway secret. Only lightweight health monitoring is publicly reachable.

Does the travel API return live ticket or fare inventory?

No. It provides planning data, official links, provider search routes, heuristic reference estimates, and scoring for real offers supplied by your application.

Are RemoteJobsAPI listings attributed?

Yes. Responses preserve the original application URL, source, and source domain so downstream products can display proper attribution.
