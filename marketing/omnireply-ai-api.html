Security and channel notes

Is it free to call directly?
No. Commercial endpoints require the paid gateway credential and direct unpaid calls return payment-required responses.
Does it send WhatsApp or Telegram messages by itself?
It generates replies and normalizes webhook payloads. Real outbound sending requires the buyer's own official Meta or Telegram channel credentials.
Why is this good for businesses?
It gives support teams multilingual reply suggestions, intent labels, sentiment, confidence, quick replies and escalation decisions without building a full bot stack from scratch.
